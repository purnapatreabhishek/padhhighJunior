Super Mentors

Children always like teachers who are friendly and teach in a fun way. The mentors encourage the student to work hard and solve more and more questions. The mentors are responsible for solving all the doubts of the students and to conduct their weekly tests to check the children’s weekly progress.

02
Brain Reflection

We encourage the students to make notes after watching each and every video and the notes have to be submitted to the teacher. This helps the teacher know whether the child is studying or not. The teacher will be in touch with the parents on a weekly basis and will give the progress report of the child.

1:1 Live Discussions

The child can take upto two 1:1 live doubt classes in a week. The child can even make a special request to the teacher to teach a particular chapter in the doubt class & get all his doubts clarified.
